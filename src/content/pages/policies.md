## Confidence before commitment

At Dazzle, every inquiry and purchase should feel considered, transparent, and supported. These policies explain how we handle value protection, certification, custom orders, delivery, and care.

## Policy highlights

### Buyback guidance

Eligible diamond jewelry may qualify for buyback guidance based on current market value, item condition, and original certification.

### Exchange guidance

Eligible pieces may be exchanged toward another design, upgrade, or custom consultation subject to final inspection and current value.

### Return window

Standard ready pieces may be eligible for return within 15 days if unused, undamaged, and accompanied by original packaging and certification.

### Shipping and delivery

Shipments are handled with care and insurance where applicable, so your jewelry reaches you securely.

### Certification and hallmarking

Where applicable, diamonds are supported by grading documentation and jewelry metal purity is hallmarked or documented.

### Custom orders

Custom work begins only after design, budget, stone, timeline, and final approval are clearly discussed.

## Detailed policies

### Buyback policy

Eligible diamond jewelry purchased from Dazzle may qualify for a buyback reference value. The final value depends on current market rates, metal and stone condition, certification availability, and any applicable making charges, taxes, or deductions.

### Exchange policy

Eligible pieces may qualify for an exchange reference value toward a new piece of equal or higher value. The final exchange value is confirmed after inspection and valuation.

### Return policy

Standard ready-to-ship items may be returned within 15 days of delivery if they are unused, unworn, undamaged, and returned with original packaging, invoices, and certificates. Custom, resized, engraved, altered, or made-to-order pieces may not be eligible for standard returns unless specifically agreed in writing.

### Shipping and delivery

We aim to provide secure, trackable, and insured shipping where available. Delivery timelines can vary by product readiness, customization, location, and logistics partner.

### Certification and hallmarking

Where applicable, diamonds are supported by grading documentation from recognized laboratories or supplier documentation. Metal purity is supported through hallmarking or appropriate documentation. We can explain certification details before purchase or consultation approval.

### Custom orders

Custom pieces are consultation-led. Before work begins, we discuss design direction, budget range, stone preference, metal choice, expected timeline, and approval steps. Any custom-order deposit, cancellation rule, or production milestone should be confirmed before proceeding.

### Warranty and maintenance

Manufacturing issues are reviewed case by case. Regular wear, impact damage, accidental breakage, misuse, loss, resizing by another jeweler, or stone damage after delivery may not be covered. We recommend periodic inspection and cleaning to preserve the piece.
