Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Dazzle handles information you share when you browse the website, submit a product inquiry, or request a consultation.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, phone number, email address, selected product, consultation preference, and message details.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to product inquiries and consultation requests.
- Discuss product availability, pricing, customization, delivery, and care questions.
- Improve our website, product information, and customer support process.
- Maintain basic inquiry records and meet applicable legal requirements.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help operate the website, process form submissions, communicate with you, or support customer service.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect submitted information. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed as completely secure.

## Your Choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete inquiry information you previously shared, subject to practical and legal retention requirements.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when website features, business processes, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be posted on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact page.
